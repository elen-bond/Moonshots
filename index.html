Our Approach

A Studio Built
for Deep Tech

We start with validated science, not ideas searching for teams. Four moves, executed in sequence, every time.

1
Source IP

We work with university research labs to find patent-protected technologies with real commercial potential, before anyone else gets there.

2
Match CEO

We recruit second-time founders from our EIR Club and pair them to IP that fits their domain expertise. Operators who've done it before, not first-timers.

3
Fund

We organize pre-seed capital fast through our EIR co-investor network, strategic angels, and family offices. Weeks, not months.

4
Scale

We bring corporate partners in from day one as pilot customers and co-investors, so the exit relationship is built in from the start.

University Partnerships

Research Ready
to Move

Universities generate some of the world's most consequential scientific breakthroughs. Most never reach the people who could benefit from them — not because the science isn't ready, but because the bridge to market doesn't exist.

Moonshots is that bridge. We work directly with tech transfer offices, identify IP with real commercial potential, and take responsibility for building the company around it.

Partner with Us
🔍

What We Look For

Patent-protected IP that gives the resulting company an exclusive market position that incumbents cannot replicate quickly.

⚡

Sectors We Focus On

Materials science, biomedical devices, industrial AI, clean energy systems, advanced sensors, and infrastructure technology.

🛡️

Risk-Free for Researchers

IP is only transferred once a company is successfully funded. If the venture doesn't raise, IP reverts to the researcher with no strings attached.

📈

Ongoing Relationship

Researchers become technical advisors in the resulting company, staying involved in commercialization while we handle operations and go-to-market.

About Moonshots

Built on a
Simple Premise.

The most consequential technologies of the next decade are already invented. They're sitting in university labs, protected by patents, validated by years of rigorous research. What they lack isn't science — it's operators, capital, and the commercial infrastructure to reach the world.

Moonshots was built to be exactly that infrastructure. We're a deep-tech venture studio: part company builder, part investor, part talent network. We're headquartered in Canada, partnered with the University of Waterloo, and building a portfolio of companies positioned to be acquired by the world's largest industrial and technology corporations.
